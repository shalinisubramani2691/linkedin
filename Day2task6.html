Bank Management System

Welcome to the Bank Management System. Here you can manage your bank accounts, view transactions, and access various banking service.

Every successful banker has to perform managerial responsibilities along with technical banking activities. Although a bank is a financial institution like other businesses, its main objective is to maximize its wealth by earning profit. Bank business is different from other types of businesses. Other business can transfer/shift their goods or services from factory to office or other far places even can export to foreign countries. But banks can transfer funds or extend services only after fulfilling the demand for a loan of the area where the office or branch of that bank is situated. Efficient management can offer high-quality service, and efficient management can be earned by efficient organization management. So, professional management is impossible without crystallizing the authority & responsibility of all the personal employed in a bank.
